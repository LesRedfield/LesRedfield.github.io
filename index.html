About Us

Natale's Pizzeria was established in 1980 and for the past 30 years has been privileged to serve Waldwick and the surrounding communities. As a family operated business we’ve always purchased the highest quality ingredients and strive to provide the best pick-up and delivery service in the industry. We are proud of our product, service and staff and we truly enjoy serving our customers. We look forward to being a part of this community and serving you for years to come.

Thanks for your patronage.
The Sernack Family
